## Recommended build priority (= Phase 3 blueprint)
1. **Branded** — already done; just confirm indexing. *(free win)*
2. **Comparison / "alternative" pages** — highest intent, lowest competition, fastest ROI. Start with **"Sell.Do alternative"** + **"Best real estate CRM for builders."**
3. **BOFU category landers** — "AI calling for real estate," "WhatsApp automation for real estate." Medium competition.
4. **MOFU/TOFU blog guides** — "how to get more site visits," "speed to lead," "why builders lose 30–40% of leads." Builds authority + top-of-funnel; great because we have real stats to cite.

## Content stats worth citing (build trust + earn AI citations)
- Leads called within 10 min are ~60% more likely to book a site visit; respond
  within 5 min = ~4x site-visit conversion; 78% of buyers choose the first
  responder. WhatsApp-first follow-up is the highest-ROI operational change.
  Builders lose ~30–40% of leads to slow/no follow-up.

## How to validate search volume (free — do before heavy writing)
1. **Google Keyword Planner** (free with a Google Ads account) — paste the
   keywords above, get volume + competition.
2. **Google Search Console** — after a few weeks of impressions, the
   Performance → Queries report shows the *real* terms you're appearing for.
3. **Google autocomplete + "People Also Ask" + "related searches"** — type a
   seed term and harvest the suggestions.
4. **Keywords Everywhere / Ahrefs free keyword generator** (freemium) — quick
   volume estimates.

## Rules
- **One primary keyword per page** (avoid cannibalization — don't make two pages
  target the same term).
- Match **intent**: comparison terms → comparison pages; "how to" → guides;
  category terms → product/landing pages.
- Write **answer-first** (the answer in the first ~150 words) so each page
  doubles as AI-search/GEO content.
