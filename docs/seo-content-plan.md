## ⚠️ Verify-before-publish (credibility + AI citations)
These ideas hinge on specific numbers. Publishing unverified stats kills trust and AI won't cite them well. Confirm real sources first (or phrase as "industry data suggests"):
- Speed-to-lead curve (10-min / 1-hr conversion figures)
- 40–60% site-visit no-show rate
- "Real estate = 0.14% of AI-search citations" claim
- 78% "first responder wins" figure

Also confirm we actually ship the features these posts showcase: **no-show recovery, LLM/AI-search placement, mid-call language switching, objection analytics.**
